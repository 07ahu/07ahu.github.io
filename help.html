DONATE

Help support important organizations that are fighting for women's rights to reproductive health and access to abortion.

01
Center for Reproductive Rights

A gift to the Center for Reproductive Rights can help "build, enforce, protect and defend legal protections for reproductive rights around the world." You can help fund the fight for abortion rights.

02
National Network of Abortion Funds

A fundraiser for 90+ abortion funds for financial help, travel, lodging and support. "Whatever the law or the courts decide, people have always had abortions and always will...We will continue working in collaboration to make abortion access possible for anyone, wherever they live, and wherever they are seeking care."

03
Keep Our Clinics

Donate to Keep Our Clinics for security improvements, equipment, clinic repairs, legal costs, paying staff, rent and for resources. "In the last six years, over 135 abortion clinics have been forced to close their doors." Also donate to Abortion Care Network for access to abortion care.

04
NARAL Pro-Choice America

Donate to NARAL Pro-Choice America to help the fight in voting for abortion rights. "We won’t forget who put our families and freedom on the line when we head to the ballot box this November. This is an all-hands-on-deck moment and we need your support to fight back for freedom today. "

05
Mariposa Fund

Help undocumented people have access to abortion. "Specifically, undocumented people face obstacles such as fear of criminalization, lack of access to health insurance, economic insecurity, linguistic barriers, and oppression rooted in white supremacy and xenophobia...Our hope is to offset the financial burden for our community’s most vulnerable members to ensure they have access to abortion care." Check out their website

06
Jane's Due Process

Jane's Due Process helps teens in Texas use their reproductive rights. They provide legal support, 1-on-1 help, and helpful information for teens to navigate reproductive health. "When you donate to Jane’s Due Process, you’re supporting Texas minors being able to exercise their reproductive rights."

07
donations4abortion

"Donate to Support Abortion Access" This website also includes a ton more organizations that you could support and also includes a lot of super important information!
